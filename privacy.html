Your Privacy is Our Priority

At PixelShrink, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our revolutionary image compression tool.

Last updated: January 2024

Information We Don't Collect

Your images NEVER leave your device

All image compression happens locally in your browser using HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere.

  • We don't collect personal information
  • We don't require registration or accounts
  • We don't track which images you compress
  • We don't have access to your files
  • We don't store compression history

Analytics and Advertising

We use the following third-party services to improve our service and display relevant advertisements:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the user experience and optimize our tool. Google Analytics may collect:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads based on your interests and previous visits to our site. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Cookies and Local Storage

Our website may use cookies and local storage for the following purposes:

  • Analytics and advertising (Google Analytics, Google AdSense)
  • Remembering your compression preferences
  • Improving user experience
  • Essential website functionality

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of our website.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.

Data Security

Since all image processing happens locally in your browser, there is no risk of data interception during transmission to our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures for our website infrastructure.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
